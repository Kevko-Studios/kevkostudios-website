Privacy Policy for Kuitar

Last updated: June 2026

1. General Information

Kuitar is a guitar learning and practice application developed by Kevko Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when using the app.

2. No User Accounts

Kuitar does not require user accounts, registration, login, or personal profile creation.

3. Local Data Storage

App settings and local user preferences may be stored on the user's device. This data is not transmitted to servers operated by Kevko Studios.

4. Microphone Access

Kuitar may request access to the device microphone for the tuner feature. Microphone input is used only to detect and analyze musical notes for tuning purposes. Audio data is processed locally on the device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to Kevko Studios.

5. Advertising

Kuitar uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and process certain data in accordance with Google's own privacy policies, including device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address information, and usage-related data required for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. The collection and processing of such data is performed by Google and governed by Google's own privacy policies.

More information about Google's privacy practices can be found here:
Google Privacy Policy

6. No Own Servers

Kevko Studios does not operate backend servers for Kuitar. No personal data is transmitted to servers operated by Kevko Studios.

7. Third-Party Services

Kuitar uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob - advertising

8. Children's Privacy

Kuitar is not specifically directed at children under the age required by applicable law. Kevko Studios does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
